Savings Account

South Indian Bank provides its customers with the option of choosing from a wide range of Savings Accounts with different features and benefits. The Savings Accounts are designed to meet the banking needs of people from all walks of life. There are special Savings Accounts for women, senior citizens and youth as well.

Each Savings Account offers unique features such as high transaction limits, free cheque books and much more. The different offers and discounts range from free subscriptions, discounted movie tickets, South Indian eDGE rewards to emergency travel allowances and a lot more. Apply for a Savings Account to enjoy all these features, offers and discounts. You can find a detailed description of the various South Indian Bank Savings Accounts below.

 Regular Savings

  • General Features

  • Accounts can be opened in joint names of family members
  • Helps you and your family to accumulate savings
  • Cheques, Drafts, Interest warrants, Dividend Warrants etc. in INR
  • First cheque book of 10 leaves free,rest chargeable as per tariff
  • Liberal withdrawal facilities
  • No Income tax deduction at source on interest
  • Standing instruction for remittance of insurance premium, rent and similar payments carried out
  • Interest will be allowed at 4% per annum or at the rate fixed by RBI from time to time
  • Nomination facility available

  • How to open an account

  • Download the application form and Fill the details, then visit nearest SIB Branch
  • Open an account with easy steps, Submit your KYC Details here.
  • Proper Introduction (you may contact nearest branch for more details)
  • You may register for the value-added services by filling up the respective forms

 Basic Savings Account

  • General Features of Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account

Eligibility Individuals in their own name or in joint names of family members
Branches authorized to open the accounts All branches except specialized branches
KYC Norms All our Basic SB accounts should be fully KYC complied accounts.
Minimum balance for opening the account Zero
Non-operation/activation charges of in-operative accounts Zero
Transactions While there will be no limit on the number of deposits that can be made in a month, maximum number of withdrawalsin a month, including ATM withdrawals is restricted to 4.If exceeded, Rs.20/- plus service tax per transaction will be charged.The individual debit and credit per transaction should be below Rs.50, 000/- for all Basic Saving Bank Deposit Accounts
ATM-cum-Debit Card Yes. Free of cost without AMC
Internet Banking / Mobile banking If the customer requires internet banking, SMS banking or other valueadded services,thecustomer has to convert the account to other category of account and keep the required minimum balances to avail the facilities.
Immediate credit of out station cheques No.
Other conditions Holders of ‘Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account’ will not be eligible for opening any other savings bank deposit account in the same bank.
If a customer has any other existing savings bank account with our bank, he/she will be required to close the account before opening a ‘Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account’.

As the name denotes, the age group is 20 years to 35 years.

  1. All students who have completed 20 years.

 SIB Smart Deposit

General Features

  • A fixed deposit linked to your CD Smart account.
  • Fixed Deposit maintained in units of Rs.10000/- Minimum Deposit Rs 50000/-Max Rs 1 Crore
  • You can link any fresh flexi deposit to your CD Smart Account
  • Fixed Deposit is broken automatically when the CD balance below Rs 25000/-

  • TDS applicable
