SIB Prime Platinum and SIB Prime

South Indian Bank Prime Platinum and Prime are exclusive Priority Banking Services offered to our High Net Worth customers which makes the relationship with the Bank mutually rewarding with benefits and offerings that enhance the privileges. As a Priority Banking customer, you have the advantage of enjoying customised benefits that recognise your total relationship with the Bank and have been tailored to suit your individual needs.

The privileges of SIB Prime Platinum and SIB Prime are not exclusively limited to the customers alone; these also get extended to your families as well. As a Priority customer, you get access to red carpet treatment at SIB Branches across the country. Also being a part of Priority Banking Program, we envisage to create a comprehensive Personal Economy Management Solution to maintain and diversify your wealth. The SIB Prime Platinum and SIB Prime Banking will be managed by exclusive Relationship Managers.
