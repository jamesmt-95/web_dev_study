 SIB Personal Loans - Rules & Regulations

Apply Online for Personal Loan
Purpose
  • Personal Use (Education, Medical, Travel, Asset Purchase, Debt consolidation etc)
  • Business Use (Working capital, Capex margin, quasi-capital, debt consolidation etc)
Target Customers
  • Salaried (Employees of Government, PSU, MNC, Listed companies and reputed
  • Public and Private limited companies only)
  • SENP (Self Employed Non-Professional) Manufacturers, Traders, Wholesalers, Retailers, Service Providers
  • SEP (Self Employed Professionals) Doctors, CA, CS, ICWA, Architects, Engineers.
Age
Minimum 21 Years
Maximum 65 Years
Amount
Minimum Rs 1.00 lacs
Maximum Rs 25.00 lacs
Tenure
Minimum 12 Months
Maximum 60 Montths
Interest rate
  • Attractive interest rate from MCLR (1 Year)+ 2.20%
  • Concession of 0.1% on eligible rates are offered to women borrowers
Eligibility
  • Minimum Bureau Score of 650 for applicant/ Co-applicant Rs. 10,000 pm Gross income ( without clubbing of income of co-borrower/guarantor) supported by ITR/Form 16/16A/Banking
  • Minimum 2 years of total employment with minimum 1 year in current employment
  • Percentage of Net take home pay after considering all repayment obligations including proposed loan reduces with increase in monthly income.
  • For Monthly income above Rs.1 lac net take home pay required is just 30%.
  • Monthly average balance computed on specific dates for a year to be at least 1.25 times proposed EMI
  • For NRI’s- Based on average monthly remittance to own NRE account or to any resident account of spouse during last 12 month excluding bulk remittances
  • Minimum 3 years of business vintage
Features
  • No security ,no collateral
  • Option for co- applicant available
  • Maximum 3 unsecured loans running including the proposed one (loans with 6 months residual tenure may be excluded).
Processing fees
  • 2% of the loan amount.
Prepayment charges
  • prepayment /part payment penalty of 4% within 1 year else after 1 year it is 2%.
